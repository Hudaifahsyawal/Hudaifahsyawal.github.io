I'm an Industrial & Systems Engineer specializing in sustainable production scheduling, multi-objective optimization, human factor engineering and AI-driven decision systems. I develop MILP models, metaheuristic optimization, and machine learning frameworks for industrial applications, with a publication record in peer-reviewed journals and a strong commitment to research and teaching.

I am currently based in **Dhahran, Saudi Arabia**, where I am a Doctor in Industrial & Systems Engineering at the **King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM)**.
